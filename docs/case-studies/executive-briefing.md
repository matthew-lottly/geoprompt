# Case Study: Executive Scenario Briefing

## Problem

A leadership team needs a fast before-and-after briefing pack that shows operational risk reduction in plain language.

## GeoPrompt workflow

1. compare baseline and candidate metrics
2. generate an executive briefing HTML pack
3. review the portfolio scorecard and scenario narrative

## KPI table to include

| KPI | Executive question answered |
| --- | --- |
| risk reduction | did the candidate actually lower the operational risk? |
| customer or demand restored | how much service improves after the investment? |
| estimated cost or effort | what is the tradeoff for the candidate option? |
| top next actions | what needs approval or funding next? |

## Narrative guidance

Use the briefing in this order:

1. what changed in the candidate scenario
2. why the change matters for service, risk, or cost
3. what leadership should approve, stage, or investigate next

## Chart annotation rules

- charts should use direct labels and named units
- every chart should explain the business meaning of improvement or decline
- executive readers should be able to understand the page without notebook code context

## Outcome

GeoPrompt turns analyst results into a briefing-ready artifact without leaving the Python workflow.
