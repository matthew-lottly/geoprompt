# Flagship Workflows

GeoPrompt stands out when the job is not just geometry handling, but explanation, scenario comparison, and action-ready reporting.

## Why choose GeoPrompt over a generic GIS dataframe?

### 1. Resilience screening
- compare baseline and candidate scenarios quickly
- export executive-ready summaries
- keep decision logic visible in code and report outputs

### 2. Utility and infrastructure operations
- route crews and restoration actions
- score asset criticality and budget priorities
- trace system impacts with business-focused metrics

### 3. Benchmark-backed reporting
- keep benchmark bundles and proof pages with releases
- show repeatable evidence instead of anecdotal speed claims

## Persona Workflow Snapshots

| Persona | Workflow | Business value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Analyst | Resilience screen | Faster option ranking and defensible comparisons |
| Executive | Briefing pack | Cleaner communication of tradeoffs and outcomes |
| Operations | Dispatch and restoration | Better crew coordination and outage response |
| Developer | Data roundtrip and packaging | Easier automation and handoff into services |
